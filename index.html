Our History and Commitment

We are second to none

Shan Marketing Services (SMS) was established in 1994 and has been able to grow every year due to a strong management and sales team. Shan Marketing Services is in the distribution business and has been engrossed in the sales and distribution of various consumer products of multinationals since its inception. Our committed partners include Pepsi Cola, Nestle Pakistan Limited, and Season Edible Oil (Wali Oil Mills). SMS has more than 800 employees and is operating more than 200 commercial vehicles of different capacities to market the products of Nestle, including Nestle F&B, Nestle Waters, Nestle Chilled Dairy, and Nestle Professionals. Furthermore, products of Pepsi-Cola and Season Oil are also distributed separately from our head office and eight different offices/warehouses based in Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Abbottabad, and Mirpur. We take pride in having established a renowned distributing company who customers can trust and be satisfied with.

Our mission is to deliver quality service and products to our customers brought in by market giants that we partner with. Thank you for checking out our website and feel free to leave us a message if you have any questions.
